# Final no-repeat campaign engine

This build permanently separates each campaign into ten levels.

- English contains 100 reviewed draft questions: 10 complete levels × 10 questions.
- Within one selected category campaign, a question ID can occur in only one level.
- Replaying a level shuffles answer order on screen, but the level keeps its assigned question set.
- Amharic currently contains 10 native questions: Level 1 is complete. Levels 2–10 intentionally show the language-content notice instead of mixing English.
- Other language question banks remain unavailable until reviewed native content is added.
- Timers now follow level progression: 10, 10, 12, 12, 15, 15, 20, 20, 25, 25 seconds.

Before public release, Bible wording, references, and Amharic translations should be reviewed by a trusted pastor or native-speaking Bible editor.
